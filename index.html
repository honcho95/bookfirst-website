Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

BookFirst ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Data stored on your device only (never sent to our servers):

  • Your reading sessions (pages read, time spent, notes)
  • Your book library (titles, authors, covers, progress)
  • Your reading schedule and preferences
  • Your reading streaks and statistics
  • Your first name (used for personalization in the app)

Data processed by third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — processes subscription purchases. Receives your Apple ID transaction data (anonymized). RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple Screen Time API — manages app blocking. We request which apps/categories to block, but we never see or store which specific apps you use. All Screen Time data stays within Apple's framework on your device.
  • Google Books API / Open Library API — when you search for or scan a book, the search query (title or ISBN) is sent to these services to find book information. No personal data is included.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your location
  • We do not access your contacts or messages
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites
  • We do not sell or share personal data with advertisers
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not store any data on external servers (all reading data is on-device only)

3. Camera & Photo Library

The camera is used solely to scan book barcodes. Photos are used solely to set custom book covers. We do not store, upload, or process camera/photo data beyond these purposes.

4. Notifications

We send local notifications (reading reminders, streak alerts, weekly summaries) only if you grant permission. These are generated on your device — no data is sent to external servers. You can disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings.

5. Children's Privacy

BookFirst is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

All reading data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Deleting the app removes all data permanently. Subscription data is managed by Apple and RevenueCat — you can manage subscriptions in Settings > Subscriptions.

7. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data on servers, there is no data to request, export, or delete from our side. All your data lives on your device and is under your control.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

Questions? Email us at support@bookfirst.app
